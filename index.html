✨ About Me ✨

Anastasia Salter is a Professor of English and Director of Graduate Programs and Texts & Technology for the College of Arts and Humanities at the University of Central Florida. Their most recent books include Playful Pedagogy in the Pandemic: Pivoting to Game-Based Learning with Emily K. Johnson (Routledge 2022) and Twining: Critical and Creative Approaches to Hypertext Narratives with Stuart Moulthrop (Amherst 2021).

Currently, Dr. Salter serves as Vice President of the Electronic Literature Organization Board of Directors.
